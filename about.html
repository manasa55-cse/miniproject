About SheCook Connect

SheCook Connect is a homemade food pre-order platform that connects homemaker women with nearby users who are looking for fresh and healthy homemade meals.

The platform allows users to explore food prepared by local homemakers and place orders in advance through a simple online system.

Instead of instant restaurant delivery, SheCook Connect follows a pre-order model where food is prepared only after orders are confirmed. This helps reduce food wastage and ensures freshness.

Users can subscribe to weekly or monthly meal plans at affordable prices and enjoy nutritious homemade food every day.

At the same time, the platform empowers homemaker women by giving them an opportunity to earn income and showcase their cooking skills.
